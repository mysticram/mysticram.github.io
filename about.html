ABOUT ME!

I'm a 15 year old Youtuber and Livestreamer from the USA! I've ran a gaming channel in the past called "MrMario1701", but it quickly transformed into my record label that I also run! I always loved games and the first video games I can remember playing were 007 Goldeneye for the Gamecube and Mario Kart 64. I've always had a love for the Mario franchise and FPS's. Once I turned 13, I was able to create a Youtube channel and thus was "MrMario1701" born! After a 2 1/2 year run, I stopped gaming and switched to something I love just as much as gaming, music! I created a music promotion channel that, in January of 2015, turned into a full-fledged record label!
